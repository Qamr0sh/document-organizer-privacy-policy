Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Document Organizer, developed by Wondelo. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data and privacy when using our application.

Data Collection and Storage

Document Organizer is currently a fully offline application. This means:

  • All your files and folders are stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect or transmit any of your data to external servers
  • Your documents remain private and under your control

Features and Data Handling

Our current features include:

  • Creating and managing folders
  • Importing and organizing files
  • Local file management

All these operations are performed locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Future Backup Functionality

We plan to introduce backup functionality in future updates. When implemented, this feature will:

  • Allow you to opt-in to backup services
  • Provide clear information about data storage locations
  • Give you full control over what data is backed up
  • Implement strong encryption for backed-up data

We will update this privacy policy when backup functionality becomes available, and you will be notified of any changes.

Your Rights

As a user of Document Organizer, you have the right to:

  • Access all your data stored in the application
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Choose whether to use future backup features
